Who am I

Hi and welcome! Nice to see you here. I'm Chris Huider, a game programmer who's hard working and eager to learn. I pride myself on writing clean, reusable and well thought out code as well as my ability to learn quick and adapt fast.

My passion

I've always been someone who can really place themselves inside a story or character and I've been able to experience and feel things through games and other media as though I had just experienced it for myself. I want to bring these kinds of experiences and feelings to everyone. I want people to live and experience games as I always have and strife to bring them the best experience possible, whether that is through writing, visual or sound design or just good programming.
